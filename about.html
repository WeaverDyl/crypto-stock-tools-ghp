About Us (Me)

This website was created by me, Dylan Weaver during the summer of 2018. It was created purely as a learning experience to learn some web development as well as Javascript skills. This website features a collection of calculators used to give you information on various cryptocurrencies. The intent is for it to be convenient to have all of these things in one place.

I'm a rising junior at the University of Maryland, College Park. I'm studying computer science and I'm also minoring in philosophy. The minor in philosophy comes from a great interest in logic and logical thinking, which is a topic that the computer science department at UMD doesn't seem to satisfy very well as a topic by itself, outside of discrete mathematics.

If you're interested in seeing more of my work, feel free to visit my Github page! I don't post much there, but many of my previous projects have been posted there, and I'm sure to post more in the future as well!
